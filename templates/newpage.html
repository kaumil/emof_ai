emof.ai 

     Emotion, Age and Gender Classification

Upload your own video and will give statistics on expressions, grouped by age

Video Processing

Convolutional Neural Network detects face and recognizes it. It classifies emotion of the person amongst 7 emotions as well as classifies the age group of the person as well as the gender.

Video Analysis Graph

The video analysis graph performs statistical data analyis from the video processing steps. A graph is given as output which gives the percentage ratio of each emotion in the video, grouped into several age-groups as well as the genders.
